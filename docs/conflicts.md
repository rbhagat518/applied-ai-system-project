# Handling Schedule Conflicts

## Conflict Resolution
- **Separate Overlapping Tasks**: If two tasks overlap, reschedule one to avoid stress on the pet.
- **Prioritize by Importance**: Health tasks (medication, feeding) should never be compromised.
- **Consider Pet's Energy**: Some pets need rest between activities.
- **Multi-Pet Households**: Allow overlapping tasks across different pets if they don't interfere.

## Common Conflicts
- **Feeding and Medication**: Medication should be given 30 minutes before or after feeding.
- **Walking and Grooming**: Grooming can be done after a walk when the pet is calmer.
- **Multiple Walks**: Space walks at least 4-6 hours apart for most dogs.

## Prevention
- Plan schedules with buffer time between tasks.
- Review weekly and adjust as needed.
- Consider pet's age and health when scheduling.
